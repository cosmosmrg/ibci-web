AboutUs

Overview

IBCI group was originally founded back in 1988 as a contractor specialized in Industrial Building, Tunneling and Waste Water Projects. Later in 2010, IBC Industrial Co., Ltd. was established as Construction Company specialized in Energy, Petrochemicals and Industrial Sectors.

We have targeted ourselves as a valued partner for Construction projects within Oil & Gas, Petrochemical and Industrial fields. We procure experiences on both on-shore and off-shore projects including those where local communities are involved.

One of our top goals when pursuing projects are to deliver high project management, quality and safety standards, and Environmental practices in order to ensure that all stakeholders are contented.

Vision/Mission /Values

IBCI’s Vision

IBCI’s vision is to be a top Engineering and Construction Company amongst Energy and Petrochemical Industry in Thailand. We strike to operate our organization with a continual development on innovative and knowledge on our specialized fields.

IBCI’s Mission

  • To be an EPC contractor in Energy Industry
  • To delivery work with the highest possible quality and standards within an agreed timeframe and specified budget and to ensure client’s satisfaction
  • To develop knowledge and ability of all employees sustainably and continually
  • To create a contented working environment for all employees
  • To develop and improve internal organizational management effectively and efficiently using new and improved innovations
  • To build new businesses in relation to our core business to distribute risks of the businesses
