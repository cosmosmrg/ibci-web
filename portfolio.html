Midline Compressor Station Project

ProjectDetails

Client                :  PTT Public Company Limited
Contractor        :  China Petroleum Pipeline Bureau.
Contract Value :  1,156 MTHB
Duration Stage :  2015 – On-going

ProjectSummary

PTT Public Company Limited have completed installation of a new offshore platform-PTT Riser Platform (PRP) in the Gulf of Thailand with topside facilities and essential utilities in association with a new 42” gas transmission pipeline which would be put into operation to deliver gas gathered from a number of offshore fields to PTT Gas Separation Plants in Rayong. However, extensive additional gas supplies are definitely to be available soon largely from developing PTTEP Arthit field plus both A-18 field and B-17 field from Malaysia-Thailand Joint Development Area (JDA) whereas other new gas fields shall be obtainable in the near future. Therefore, in order to meet increasing capacity to deliver more substantial gas volume, three new PCS Turbine/Compressor units with pertinent facilities are inevitably required on PTT Riser Platform (PRP).


The major equipments of the Project on PRP with operating conditions are followings:
• PCS Gas Turbine/ Compressor
• Aftercoolers
• PCF Turbine Gernerator


The normal flow rate required with Platform Compression facilities on PRP through the transmission 42” gas Pipeline is about 1,900 MMscfd while pipeline inlet pressure from PRP is to be maintained at 2,100 psig and the multiple incoming gas sources to PRP from Arthit, JDA and ERP shall be regulated at 1,100 psig. The total flow of 1,900 MMscfd shall be normally handled by 2 Compressor Units with a 50% capacity whereas the third unit shall be normally for standby purpose. Each Compressor unit shall be driven by a Gas Turbine. The driver shall have adequate margin at site conditions to meet the compression duty.The Gas Turbine speed shall be varied depending upon the flow and discharge pressure required. It is normally expected that the Platform Compression Facilities Project: PCF discharge pressure required shall vary the speed of the machine with suction pressure override.


scope of work

  • Engineering, Procurement and Construction of the main control building
  • Main Civil Work and Site Preparation including landscape work for all project areas
  • Equipment Installation
  • Fabrication and Installation of 2,000 Tons of Structural Steel
  • Installation of Electrical and Instrumentation Work
  • Pre-commissioning, and assist during commissioning

Key Challenges

  • LEEDs Certification of the main control building as Green Building
  • Wireless control of electrical system in compliance to KNX Standard

Onshore Compressor Station #3 Project

ProjectDetails

Client                :  PTT Public Company Limited
Contractor        :  Man Ferrostaal Industrieanlagen GmbH
Contract Value :  878 MTHB
Duration Stage :  2005 – 2007

ProjectSummary

The Onshore Compressor Station #3 (OCS #3) Project is to be constructed as a local and remote Compressor Station adjacent to the third transmission pipeline onshore facilities. The compressor station is to be consisted on the following:

• Inlet scrubbers with filters
• Fuel gas treatment system
• Gas turbine – Compressor Package
• Gas cooler
• Station Piping
• Utility System and shelters


All of the above shall be equipped with instruments, control system and other equipment required for the local and remote control operation of the compressor station.


Suction and discharge piping headers of the new compressor station shall be tied to the existing process block valve of 42” and 36”, respectively. Both block valves shall be above ground and isolated from the existing buried pipe headers using mono block type isolation joint. The electrical power supply of the station shall be taken from switchgear of 6.9 kV at a substation.


scope of work

  • Site Preparation and Civil Work
  • Control and other Building Works
  • Installation of 3 units of Gas Turbine Compressor, Inlet Scrubber, Gas Cooler, Flare
  • Fabrication & Installation of Steel Structure & Piping Work
  • Electrical & Instrumentation Work
  • Ties-in Work
  • Pre-commissioning and assist during Commissioning Work

Key Challenges

  • Brown field working conditions in some area
  • Short execution work period (16.5 months)

Platform Compressor Facility Project

ProjectDetails

Client                :  PTT Public Company Limited
Contractor        :  Punj Llyod Group JV
Contract Value :  $31M
Duration Stage :  2010 – 2014

ProjectSummary

After the completion of a new PTT Riser Platform (PRP) in the Gulf of Thailand, a new 42” gas transmission pipeline is to be operated for the delivery of gas gathered from a number of offshore fields to PTT Gas Separation Plant in Rayong.


Due to a rapid development of PTTEP Arthit field plus (both A-18 and B-17) and a Joint Development Area (JDA) between Malaysia and Thailand, three units of a new PCS Turbine/Compressors with pertinent facilities are inevitably required on the PTT Riser Platform (PRP), in order to meet up with the increasing capacity in a delivery of substantial gas volume.


Major equipment required for the PRP Project is PCS Gas Turbine/ Compressor, After-coolers and PCF Turbine Generator. The required flow-rate for Gas Compressor through the transmission of a 42” Gas Pipeline is at about 1,900 MMscfd, while the inlet pressure from PRP is to be maintained at 2,1000 psig. The incoming gas sources from Arthit and JDA shall be regulated at 1,100 psig. Two Compressors units with a 50% capacity are to handle the flow of 1,900 MMscfd, whereas the third compressor is for stand-by purpose only.

A gas turbine is used to drive each compressor units, where the margin is at an adequate level at site conditions to meet the compression duty. The speed of gas turbine shall be varied according to the flow and discharge pressure required.


scope of work

  • Fabrication and Installation of 3 Modules of Gas Turbine Compressor
  • Fabrication & Installation of Steel Structure & Piping Work
  • Electrical & Instrumentation Work
  • Sea Fastening and Load Out
  • Pre-commissioning
  • Offshore supply of manpower, tools & equipment, scaffolding, and supply vessel

Key Challenges

  • Offshore working condition
  • Brownfield working Environment
  • Working with High Pressure, i.e. 2,400 psi standard
